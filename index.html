Blog Posts

About to Launch an 8-Part Series: AskSense from Conception to Deployment

April 29, 2025

We turned AskSense’s FAQ search into a real-time scam-detection pipeline after keyword filters proved too noisy. Incoming messages are embedded via multilingual SBERT, then classified or similarity-thresholded against known high-risk patterns, with unsupervised clustering to catch new tactics. We solved Chinese segmentation with custom Jieba dictionaries and OpenCC, slashed cold-start times via incremental caching, and added FAISS for millisecond lookups. A CI/CD-backed Streamlit deployment, A/B testing, and feedback loops ensure continuous precision-recall tuning and adaptation to evolving phishing strategies.
