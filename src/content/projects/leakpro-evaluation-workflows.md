## Framing

This repository is a fork-based adaptation study rather than an original tool built from scratch. I include it because it reflects an important part of my profile: careful evaluation of security claims around deployed AI systems.

## Why it matters

Trustworthy AI is not only about the model's output quality. It is also about what the model can leak, what assumptions it depends on, and how security concerns are surfaced before deployment.

## What this case study represents

LeakPro Evaluation Workflows is best understood as a research instrument: a way of developing stronger judgment around privacy risk, reproducibility, and how to reason about attack surfaces in applied ML.
