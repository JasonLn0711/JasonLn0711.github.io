## Why include an exploration entry

Not every meaningful technical project is a polished product. Some matter because they build intuition around new model classes, tooling assumptions, and the gap between research excitement and deployable systems.

## What this exploration is about

This repository is a fork-based exploration of a text-audio foundation model. It helps me examine model behavior, interface patterns, and how emerging audio generation capabilities might intersect with speech systems work more broadly.

## Portfolio value

I include this kind of experiment because it shows technical curiosity paired with restraint. The goal is to learn enough to make better architectural decisions later, not to overstate what a borrowed codebase means.
