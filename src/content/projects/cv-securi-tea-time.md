## What it does

CV_Securi-Tea-Time is a practical security pipeline for detecting people and vehicles, extracting license information, logging events, and sending near-real-time alerts with evidence snapshots.

## System value

The interesting part is not just the use of models. It is the end-to-end workflow:

1. Capture a live feed
2. Detect relevant objects
3. Enrich events with OCR and simple identity cues
4. Save reviewable records and push alerts

## Why it matters here

This project predates the current doctoral research direction, but it already shows the pattern that continues through my later work: building intelligence systems that are judged by operational usefulness, not only by isolated model output.
